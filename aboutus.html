Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my air conditioner?

We recommend servicing your air conditioning system at least once a year to ensure optimal performance and efficiency.

What should I do if my AC stops cooling?

Check your thermostat settings, ensure your filters are clean, and if the issue persists, contact our expert technicians for assistance.

How can I improve my AC's energy efficiency?

Regular maintenance, cleaning the filters, and ensuring your home is well-insulated can significantly improve energy efficiency.

What size air conditioner do I need for my home?

The ideal size depends on factors like room size, ceiling height, and insulation. Contact us for a professional assessment to ensure you get the right unit.

Why is my air conditioner making unusual noises?

Unusual sounds may indicate loose parts, debris buildup, or motor issues. It's best to schedule a technician visit for a thorough inspection.
